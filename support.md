## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**What is LensLingo?**
LensLingo is a travel translation app that lets you translate text using your camera, voice, or keyboard across 50+ languages.

**Does LensLingo require an account?**
No. LensLingo works without any login or account.

**Which languages are supported?**
LensLingo supports 50+ languages. Language detection is available automatically when you set the source to "Detect language."

---

### Camera & OCR

**How do I translate text from a photo?**
Open the Camera tab, set your target language, then either point your camera at text or import a photo from your library. Tap a text region or use full-page scan.

**Can I import PDFs or files?**
Yes. You can import photos, PDFs, and text files from your device for OCR translation.

**Why is the camera text recognition inaccurate?**
OCR accuracy depends on image quality, lighting, and font style. For best results, ensure the text is well-lit, in focus, and not at a steep angle.

---

### Voice & Conversation

**How do I use voice translation?**
Go to the Voice tab, choose your source and target languages, then tap the microphone and speak. The app will transcribe and translate your speech.

**What is Conversation mode?**
Conversation mode enables two-way translation between two people speaking different languages. An optional group mode supports three languages simultaneously.

---

### Widgets & Extensions

**How do I add a widget to my Home Screen?**
Long-press your Home Screen, tap the + button, search for LensLingo, and choose your preferred widget size.

**How do I use the Share Extension?**
Select any text or image in another app, tap Share, and choose LensLingo to translate it directly.

---

### Settings & Data

**How do I clear my translation history or learned phrases?**
Go to Settings inside the app and tap Clear AI Memory / Clear Learned Phrases.

**Is my data stored on the cloud?**
No. Translations, learned phrases, favorites, and settings are stored on your device only.

**What permissions does the app need?**
- Camera — to scan text in your environment
- Microphone — for voice translation
- Photo Library — to import images you select
- Speech Recognition — to transcribe speech to text
- Notifications — for optional alerts (can be denied)

You can manage all permissions in iOS Settings > LensLingo.

---

### Subscriptions & Purchases

**Is LensLingo free?**
The free tier includes a set number of translations per language. Subscriptions may be offered in future updates.

**How do I restore a purchase?**
Use the Restore Purchases option inside the app under Settings or the subscription screen.

**Purchases are handled by Apple.** For billing issues, visit [Apple Support](https://support.apple.com/billing).

---

### Troubleshooting

**The app is crashing. What should I do?**
1. Force-close the app and reopen it.
2. Make sure your iOS is up to date.
3. Delete and reinstall the app.
4. If the issue persists, email us at muzaffarumar768@gmail.com with your device model and iOS version.

**Translation results seem wrong.**
LensLingo uses machine translation and results may not always be perfect. For critical communication, always verify with a qualified human interpreter.

**A permission is missing or denied.**
Go to iOS Settings > LensLingo and enable the required permission. Some features will not work without their corresponding permissions.
