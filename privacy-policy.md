# LensLingo Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

LensLingo ("we", "our", "the app") helps you translate text using your camera, voice, keyboard, and imported files. This policy explains what information is processed when you use the app.

## Information we process

- **Text you translate** — words you type, speak, photograph, or import from photos, PDFs, or text files.
- **Images** — photos from your camera or photo library when you choose to scan or import them for OCR.
- **Audio** — microphone input when you use voice translation (processed on-device by Apple speech recognition, then sent for translation if needed).
- **Language preferences** — selected source/target languages, settings, and on-device translation history.
- **Corrections you teach** — phrase corrections you save with "Not quite right?" stored on your device.
- **Diagnostics** — crash logs and basic usage analytics via Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics (if enabled in your build).
- **Push token** — if you enable notifications, a device token is used to deliver messages via Apple Push Notification service and Firebase Cloud Messaging.

## How we use information

We use this data only to provide translation, improve reliability, and operate app features (widgets, share extension, shortcuts). We do not sell your personal information.

## Third-party services

To translate text, the app may send content to:

- Apple on-device translation (iOS 18+ where available)
- Google Translate endpoints
- MyMemory (Translated.net)
- Lingva mirror services
- Firebase (Google) for analytics, crash reporting, and push messaging
- CountAPI — only an anonymous install counter for optional launch promotions

These providers process text you submit for translation. Do not submit passwords, payment card numbers, or other highly sensitive data.

## On-device storage

Recent translations, learned phrases, favorites, trip countdown, and settings are stored on your device. You can clear learned memory in Settings.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — scan text in the environment
- **Microphone** — voice translation
- **Photo library** — import images you select
- **Speech recognition** — transcribe speech to text
- **Notifications** — optional alerts

## Children

LensLingo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Your choices

- Deny permissions in iOS Settings (some features will not work)
- Clear AI memory in Settings
- Disable push notifications
- Delete the app to remove on-device data
